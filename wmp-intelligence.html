Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) system for Filsinger Energy Partners to analyze and benchmark Wildfire Mitigation Plans across different regions. The system leverages advanced AI technologies to provide strategic intelligence on regional trends and enhance client advisement capabilities.
